About Our Los Angeles Service

LA is where we live.

Los Angeles is our home base — the city where Accusharp started and where we know every back road to every neighborhood. We've been sharpening for LA home cooks, restaurants, food trucks, and private chefs for over two decades.

Because LA is our home turf, we offer faster service here than anywhere else. Same-day appointments are frequently available when you call before noon. LA Questions

Los Angeles FAQs

How fast can you get to my LA address?
LA is our most frequent route — we're somewhere in the city most days of the week. Same-week booking is standard; same-day is often possible if you call before noon.
Do you do food trucks and private chefs?
Absolutely. Food trucks, catering kitchens, and private chef clients are a big part of our LA business. We offer recurring routes at commercial rates — some LA clients have been with us for 10+ years.
Is there a travel fee for farther LA neighborhoods?
All of LA proper is in our core zone — no travel fee. Only far valley locations (north of Van Nuys) or distant Westside addresses occasionally carry a small charge. We'll always confirm before booking.
What about Japanese knives? LA has a lot.
Yes — we sharpen Japanese knives regularly. Shun, Global, yanagibas, deba, usuba — just mention it in the booking notes so we bring the right stones. LA customers tend to have some of the best knife collections, and we treat them accordingly.
Do you service apartment buildings in LA?
Yes. For apartments, we'll coordinate with you on where to meet — lobby, parking garage, or curbside. Just mention the building type in your booking notes.
Can you sharpen at my LA restaurant during service hours?
We usually schedule restaurant visits during prep hours (9am-11am) or off-hours (2pm-4pm). Whatever fits your service windows. We can also come in before first prep if that works better.
